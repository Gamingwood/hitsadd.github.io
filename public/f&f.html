Facts About Cinema

First Feature Film: "The Story of the Kelly Gang" (1906) is often considered the world's first feature film. It was over an hour long and depicted the story of the Australian outlaw Ned Kelly.

Most Profitable Movie Ever: "Paranormal Activity" holds the record for the most profitable film ever made, given its tiny budget of $15,000 and enormous box office earnings.

Highest-Grossing Film: As of my last update, "Avengers: Endgame" holds the record for the highest-grossing film of all time, earning over $2.798 billion worldwide.

First CGI Character: The first entirely computer-generated character to appear in a movie was in the film "Young Sherlock Holmes" in 1985. It was a stained-glass knight that came to life.

Most Expensive Film: "Pirates of the Caribbean: On Stranger Tides" holds the record for the most expensive film ever made, with a budget of around $379 million.

First Movie With Sound: "The Jazz Singer," released in 1927, was the first feature-length film with synchronized dialogue. It marked the transition from silent films to "talkies."

Shortest Film: "Napoleon," made by Abel Gance in 1927, holds the record for the shortest film ever publicly screened. It lasted just 4 minutes.

Longest Film: The longest film ever made is "Logistics," released in 2012, with a runtime of 51 days and 7 hours. It's a real-time depiction of the production and transportation of goods.

Box Office Champion: As of my last update, "Avatar" held the title of the highest-grossing film of all time. It was directed by James Cameron and released in 2009, earning over $2.8 billion worldwide.

Longest Film: The longest film ever made is "Logistics," released in 2012, with a runtime of 51 days and 7 hours. It's a real-time depiction of the production and transportation of goods.

Long Production Timeline: The development and production of "Avatar" spanned many years. James Cameron reportedly worked on the script for nearly a decade before the actual filming began.

Culmination of the MCU: "Avengers: Endgame" served as the culmination of the Marvel Cinematic Universe's Infinity Saga. It tied together storylines from multiple films and brought a resolution to the Thanos storyline.

Record-Breaking Opening: It had an enormous opening weekend, setting records for box office revenue and drawing huge crowds globally.
